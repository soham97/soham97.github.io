Soham Deshmukh

Applied Scientist, Scheduler, Microsoft

I am an applied scientist at Microsoft. I am interested in weakly supervised learning with applications in audio scene understanding and task oriented natural language understanding. Work research page here: Soham at MSR

I received my masters in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Carnegie Mellon University where my research was advised by Prof. Bhiksha Raj and Prof. Rita Singh at MLSP. Prior to CMU, I received my B.Tech from Department of Electrical Engineering, VJTI, where I worked with Prof. Faruk Kazi in the area of threat modelling and natural language processing.

Professionally, I spent my summers interning at Microsoft, Siemens R&D, Siemens and winning some Kaggle medals in competitions by Google, Quora.
